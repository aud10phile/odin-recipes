Morning Smoothie of the Gods

Description

My morning usually consists of a big smoothie to start my day. It helps get a good couple servings of greens (especially the leafy ones) and I can get fruit in there as well. This is a recipe that I started making while on a gut reset program and it was the quick and easy solution to having breakfast on the go.

I use a somewhat high powered blender for this and I would recommend having something along those lines for your ease of mind. I also tried making something similar before this blender and it did not go well. RIP. I also have all of my ingredients frozen (save the coconut milk and collagen powder), this saves me from having to add ice cubes which dilutes the taste.

Ingredients

  • Power greens(think kale, spinach, chard)
  • Berry blend(I like to have strawberries in the mix because they add sweetness)
  • Collagen powder
  • Coconut milk(something with no added sugar)

Directions

  1. Measure out your greens to roughly 2 cups
  2. Add the greens to the blender
  3. Add coconut milk to the top of the greens
  4. Scoop your collagen powder on top of the greens and milk
  5. Put one final cup of greens on top and to top it off, add your berries.
  6. To give a good consistency, have your coconut milk to the top of the berries
  7. Blend until smooth.
